ORDINAL

Ordinal is a group of Informatics students at UKIM who love to play games. This group was founded in the UKIM computer lab in 2019, with the same love for the mobile game 'Mobile Legends'.

Since its inception, Ordinal has experienced many changes in its members, but the core group of five people has always been together through thick and thin.

Although it is only a small group that plays for fun, Ordinal participates in online and offline tournaments, showing off their abilities on a larger scale.

Currently, all of its members have graduated and are still in touch with each other. We all still hang out occasionally while eating fried chicken together and playing other games like Tekken or PES.

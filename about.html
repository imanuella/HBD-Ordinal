ORDINAL

Welcome to the About Page of Ordinal, a group of students at UKIM who are passionate about Informatics and gaming.

The group was founded in the computer lab of UKIM in 2019, with a shared love for playing the mobile game Mobile Legends.

Since its inception, Ordinal has seen many changes in its members, but a core group of five members has remained dedicated to the group and its mission.

Despite changes in membership, the group has continued to thrive and excel in its gaming pursuits.

One of the key games that the group enjoys playing is Mobile Legends, and all members have achieved the coveted Mythic rank with ease.

This level of skill has enabled the group to participate in both online and offline tournaments, showcasing their abilities on a larger scale.


As the years have passed, the chemistry between the five members has only grown stronger, cementing their bond as a cohesive and dedicated gaming group.

With a love for gaming and a passion for Informatics, the members of Ordinal continue to push boundaries and achieve new heights in their pursuits.
